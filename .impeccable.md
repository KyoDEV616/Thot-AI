## Design Context

### Users
Solo developer / power user using a local AI assistant on macOS. Used in a focused, intentional work session — often in dark environments. The app is their private thinking companion: they want depth, not friendliness theater.

### Brand Personality
**Wise · Mysterious · Refined**
Ancient and deliberate. Like a scholar's tool — not a consumer app. Should feel like something rare and carefully made, not like yet another AI chatbot wrapper.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark by default. A focused workspace tool used at a desk, often at night.
- **Reference**: Linear / Arc Browser. Extreme refinement, premium dark mode, micro-details. Every pixel feels intentional.
- **Anti-references**: ChatGPT (too generic), Notion (too light and playful), any purple-glow "AI aesthetic" with gradient text.
- **Palette**: Deep purples and golds (Pyramid theme) — evokes ancient knowledge, not tech-bro cool.
- **Font**: Bricolage Grotesque — editorial grotesque with subtle character tension between organic and mechanical. Fits "wise + precise" without feeling cold.

### Design Principles
1. **Deliberate over busy** — Every animation earns its presence. Nothing decorative.
2. **Premium stillness** — Silence between interactions should feel calm, not dead.
3. **Reveal sophistication** — The interface teaches itself through interaction, not upfront.
4. **Egyptian subtlety** — The theme is a whisper, not a costume. Ancient without cosplay.
5. **No AI slop tells** — No gradient text, no glowing borders, no bounce easing.

### Motion Philosophy
**Premium fluido** — every action gets deliberate feedback.
- Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` (ease-out-expo) for entrances
- Exit: ~75% of enter duration
- Never bounce, never elastic
- `prefers-reduced-motion` always respected
